where are we — shows the New Jersey municipality you are technically in, resolved against the state's official boundary polygons (NJOGIS "Municipal Boundaries of NJ", all 564 municipalities), and keeps a list of every town you've visited. The boundary data downloads once and is cached on this device; after that, the answer works offline. The map needs a connection for its base tiles; the boundary lines and your dot do not. Tap the big town name to center the map on yourself; tap a visited row to pan to that town. "days" counts distinct calendar days present. Outside New Jersey it reports coordinates only. Data: NJOGIS / NJGIN Open Data. Map tiles © OpenStreetMap contributors.
